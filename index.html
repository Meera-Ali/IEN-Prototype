How it works

Four steps to your ride

01

Post your pickup

Enter your pickup location and when your class starts. A few hours ahead is best — gives time for a match.

02

Requests go live

Nearby verified students heading in a similar direction can see your request — but no personal details yet.

03

A driver accepts

A student-driver already heading your way accepts. You see their name, car type, experience, and reviews.

04

Confirm & go

Both sides confirm and pay if applicable. The cost is calculated and split based on your locations.
