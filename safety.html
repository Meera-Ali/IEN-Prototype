Safety & Trust

How Meshwar keeps every student safe

🆘

Emergency contact

Feel unsafe during a ride? Alert campus security and your emergency contact instantly.

University verification

Every user must sign up with an active university email — the same credentials used for iLearn and other institutional systems. Random or personal emails are rejected. This ensures only current students can access the platform.

Gender separation

The app separates users by gender. Female users only see female drivers, and male users only see male drivers. This is a hard platform rule — no override is possible — to ensure every user rides comfortably and safely.

License verification

All drivers must upload a valid UAE driving license during signup. Our team reviews every submission before a driver can accept rides. Licenses are checked against the provided university ID to prevent impersonation.

Privacy by default

When a rider posts a request, no personal information is visible to drivers — only the area and time. Full details (first name, car info, plate number) are only shared after both parties explicitly confirm the ride.

Ratings & accountability

After every ride, both the rider and driver can rate each other. Accounts with consistently low ratings are flagged for review. Repeated poor behaviour results in permanent suspension from the platform.

In-app route tracking

The agreed pickup and destination are locked before the ride starts. Riders can see the active route in-app throughout the journey. Any significant deviation triggers an automatic safety check-in notification.

Community guidelines

✓

Be on time. If you request a ride, be at your pickup location when agreed. Repeated no-shows affect your account.

✓

Respect personal space. Keep conversation respectful. No personal questions or inappropriate behaviour.

✓

Honest reviews. Leave accurate ratings to help the community trust each other.

✗

No account sharing. Your account is personal. Letting others use it is grounds for permanent ban.

✗

No cash outside the app. All payments go through Meshwar. Asking for cash separately violates our policy.

🚩

Report a concern

Uncomfortable during a ride? Seen something suspicious? Let us know. All reports are confidential.
